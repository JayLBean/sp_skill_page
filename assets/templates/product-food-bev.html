The details

Tasting notes & how to brew it

Tasting notes & roast profile +
LightDark

Roasted light to protect the delicate fruit that natural processing brings out. Expect a syrupy body and a juicy, wine-like acidity.

  • Aroma — blueberry, ripe strawberry
  • Flavor — blueberry compote, jasmine, stone fruit
  • Finish — clean, sweet, lingering florals
  • Body — syrupy · Acidity — bright
Brew guide +

Dialed in by our roasters. Start here, then adjust grind to taste.

  • Pour-over — 22 g coffee : 360 g water, 96°C, medium grind, 3:00 total brew
  • French press — 30 g coffee : 500 g water, coarse grind, 4:00 steep
  • Espresso — 18 g in : 40 g out, fine grind, 28 seconds

Best enjoyed within 3–4 weeks of the roast date printed on your bag.

Origin & sourcing +

Grown at 1,900–2,100m by smallholder farmers around the Guji zone of Oromia, Ethiopia, and dried on raised beds for up to three weeks. Heirloom varieties, natural process.

Bought direct at prices set above Fair Trade, from the same washing station year after year.
