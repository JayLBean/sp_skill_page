Orders & shipping

4 articles
When will my order ship? +

Orders placed before 1pm ET on a business day are packed and shipped the same day. Everything else goes out the next business day. You'll get a confirmation email with tracking the moment your parcel leaves our Toronto studio.

How long does delivery take? +

Standard delivery is 2–5 business days across Canada and the continental US. Expedited (1–2 day) options are available at checkout. Remote areas may add a day or two.

Do you ship internationally? +

Yes — we ship to 30+ countries. International orders typically arrive in 6–12 business days. Any customs duties or import taxes are calculated and shown at checkout, so there are no surprises at your door.

Is shipping really carbon-neutral? +

It is. We measure the emissions of every shipment and fund verified reforestation and clean-energy projects to offset them. Our mailers and cartons are curbside-recyclable, and we never use plastic void fill.

Returns

4 articles
What is your return policy? +

Every order is covered by our 30-day happiness guarantee. If your skin isn't glowing, return the product within 30 days of delivery for a full refund — even if the bottle is empty. No return shipping required.

How do I start a return? +

Head to our returns portal, enter your order number and email, and pick the items you'd like to send back. We'll email a prepaid label and issue your refund as soon as it's scanned by the carrier.

Can I return a product I've already opened? +

Absolutely. We'd rather you try a formula properly than send it back unused. Opened, sampled, and even fully finished products are all eligible under the 30-day guarantee.

When will I get my refund? +

Refunds are issued to your original payment method within 3–5 business days of your return being scanned. Your bank may take an extra day or two to post it to your statement.

Ingredients

4 articles
Are your products vegan and cruelty-free? +

Every Lumina formula is 100% vegan and certified cruelty-free. We never test on animals and never ask anyone else to. No beeswax, lanolin, carmine, or animal-derived ingredients — ever.

Do you disclose full ingredient doses? +

Yes — radical transparency is the whole point. We print the percentage of every active on the box, and the complete INCI list with each ingredient's function is on every product page. No "fragrance" black boxes.

Are your formulas fragrance-free? +

All of our treatment products are formulated without added fragrance, essential oils, or dyes, so they're suited to sensitive and reactive skin. Any product with scent is clearly labelled on the front of the pack.

Are your products safe to use during pregnancy? +

Most of our range is pregnancy-friendly, but a few actives (like retinoids) are best paused. We flag these on each product page. As always, we recommend checking with your doctor about your specific routine.

Skin advice

4 articles
How do I know which products are right for me? +

Take our 2-minute skin quiz — it asks about your skin type, concerns, and current routine, then builds a personalised regimen. Prefer a human? Our care team offers free routine consultations by email.

In what order should I layer my products? +

A simple rule: thinnest to thickest. In the morning, cleanse, apply your vitamin C serum, moisturise, then finish with SPF. At night, cleanse, treat, and seal with a barrier cream or overnight mask.

Can I use vitamin C and retinol together? +

You can, but we recommend splitting them: vitamin C in the morning for antioxidant defence, retinol at night for renewal. Introduce one new active at a time and build up frequency slowly to keep your barrier happy.

How long until I see results? +

Skin renews on roughly a 28-day cycle, so give any new active at least four weeks of consistent use. That's exactly why our formulas are backed by a 28-day promise — see a visible difference or your money back.
