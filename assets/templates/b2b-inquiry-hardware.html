Before you send an RFQ

Common questions

Quick answers on minimums, lead times, and terms. Anything specific to your parts, put it in the message field above.

What is your minimum order quantity (MOQ)? +

Most stocked hardware has low minimums — often 25–100 pcs, and many fasteners sell in standard box quantities. There's no minimum dollar value to open a quote.

Custom and made-to-print work carries a setup-driven MOQ that depends on the process (typically 250–1,000 pcs for CNC, higher for stampings). We'll state it clearly on your quote.

How fast can you ship? +

Stocked SKUs ship the same day when ordered by 3:00 pm ET from any of four regional hubs. Standard lead time is one business day to most of the continental US.

Custom and made-to-print parts run on committed dates — usually 2–3 weeks, quoted firm rather than "estimated." Expedite and AOG service is available on request.

Do you offer net terms? +

Yes — net-30 is available on approved credit, with net-45 and blanket-order programs for established accounts. New customers can prepay by card, ACH, or wire while an application is processed.

Complete a short credit application and we typically approve terms within 2–3 business days.

Can I get material certs and traceability? +

Absolutely. We supply EN 10204 3.1 material certificates, RoHS/REACH declarations, and full lot traceability with shipment. First-article inspection reports and PPAP documentation are available on request — just note the requirement on your RFQ.

Can you quote from a BOM or a drawing? +

Yes — that's the fastest path to an accurate quote. Attach a spreadsheet BOM, a PDF/DWG drawing, or STEP model above, and we'll return line-by-line pricing with substitutions flagged where a stocked equivalent saves cost or lead time.
