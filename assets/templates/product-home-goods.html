Care & origin +

Wool is naturally odour- and stain-resistant, so it needs washing far less often than you'd think — a good airing usually does it.

  • Machine wash cold on the wool cycle, or hand wash
  • Use a mild wool detergent; never bleach or tumble dry
  • Dry flat, reshaping the fringe by hand
  • Cool iron on the reverse if needed; store folded, not hung
