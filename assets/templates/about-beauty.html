Our story

Skincare built in a lab, not a boardroom.

We're Lumina — two chemists who got tired of marketing dressed up as science. This is why we exist, what we stand for, and who we're building for.

Our mission
To make proof-backed skincare that's honest about what's inside — and generous about why it works.
Written on our lab wall since day one.
The founders

Two chemists, one shared frustration.

We met over a lab bench, formulating actives for brands that spent more on packaging claims than on the formula inside. Every launch promised the moon; almost none printed a single dose you could verify.

So we built the brand we wished existed. Lumina started with a simple rule we still hold: if an ingredient can't earn its place at a level shown to work in peer-reviewed research, it doesn't go in the bottle — and whatever does goes right on the box.

Dr. Nadia VanceCo-founder · Cosmetic Chemist
Elias Moreau, M.Sc.Co-founder · Formulation Science
What we stand for

Three promises we won't compromise

How we got here

A short history of Lumina

2019

The first batch

Nadia and Elias formulate the original Barrier Repair Serum on a shared lab bench — and print every dose on the label.

2020

Lumina launches

We ship our first 500 bottles direct-to-door. They sell out in nine days, entirely by word of mouth.

2022

50,000 five-star reviews

A community of sensitive-skin customers pushes us past 50K verified reviews and our first dermatologist partnerships.

2024

Carbon-neutral, end to end

Every order ships carbon-neutral and our refill program keeps 40 tonnes of packaging out of landfill each year.

2026

Still printing every dose

Twelve products, one unchanged rule. If it can't earn its place in the formula, it doesn't go in the bottle.
