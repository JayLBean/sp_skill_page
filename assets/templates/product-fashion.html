Details & fit +

A relaxed, straight-cut silhouette with a softly built shoulder and a concealed two-button placket. Falls just past the knee; welt pockets at the hip and a single interior pocket.

  • Regular fit — size down for a sharper line
  • Model is 5'10" / 178cm and wears a size S
  • Fully lined; back vent for movement
Materials & care +

Shell: 82% virgin wool, 18% recycled polyamide woven from Italian deadstock melton. Lining: 100% recycled cupro.

Dry clean only. Steam to refresh between wears; store on a broad hanger to hold the shoulder.
