Craftsmanship & care

Carved, cast, and hallmarked in our atelier — never outsourced, never rushed.

Materials & hallmark +

Every Solstice Signet is struck with the full London Assay Office hallmark and our maker's mark.

  • Solid 18k recycled gold — 750 parts per thousand, never plated or filled
  • Weight — approx. 9.4g in size M
  • Face — 12mm softly domed, ready for seal engraving
  • Traceable metal — refined from certified recycled sources
The making +

Each piece begins as hand-carved wax, cast in small batches by a single goldsmith, then filed, stone-set where needed, and polished over two days of finishing.

Because every ring is made to order in your chosen metal and size, no two leave the bench on an assembly line.

Sizing & fit +

Not sure of your size? Order our complimentary ring sizer, or bring an existing ring to any appointment.

Your first resizing within the first year is on us — signets can be adjusted up or down by two sizes without affecting the engraving.

Shipping & returns +

Complimentary insured shipping worldwide, presented in our signature gift box. Made-to-order pieces ship in 3–4 weeks; engraved orders add 3–5 days.

Unengraved pieces may be returned within 30 days for a full refund. Bespoke and engraved commissions are final sale but always covered by our lifetime repair guarantee.
